COOL DRIP WEBSITE

1. WEBSITE SPECIFICATION
   The primary objective the COOL DRIPS website is to provide a seamless e-commerce Website for fashion-forward individuals to discover and purchase trending clothes.
   Business Goal 1: Establish a strong online brand identity that resonates with youth culture.
	Business Goal 2: Provide a frictionless mobile shopping experience.
	Business Goal 3: Build a loyal community by integrating social media "drops" and trend updates.

TARGET AUDIANCE:
Demographics: Millennials (Age between 16–40), primarily urban dwellers with an interest in streetwear and fast fashion clothes.
Technical Aptitude: Users are expected to be comfortable with digital payments, and likely to use high-end smartphones or modern browsers.

WEBSITE TYPE AND CONTENT
 This will be a Transactional E-commerce Website. Content will include high-resolution product photography, short-form promotional videos and a blog section for     style guides.
